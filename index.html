FAQ

What is included with a Pro Membership?

Unlimited access to all Bible Talk Lessons, advanced Bible Studies, Family Devotionals, Hot Topics, Sermon Illustrations, Bible Insights, and more.  

Why are you now charging money for site content?

EvanBartholomew.com has been around for over five years, and hundreds have used the resources on my site for their own ministries. It has required a significant amount of effort to provide these resources, and to maintain this website; as well as several hundreds of dollars per year.  To account for this effort, and these costs, I have decided to require a small yearly fee. 

Can I download a post without committing to a year-long membership?

Yes, you may purchase any post you like at the following address:  https://evanbartholomew.com/buy-a-post/

Can I share these posts with others?

The creative content found on this website is not to be shared with anyone who has not purchased a membership at this site.

How often do you update or add content?

I try to add content at least once a week. 

Do you offer refunds or returns on purchases?

Unfortunately, I am unable to offer refunds or returns since users have unlimited access to my site upon signing up for a membership.  

What makes your site content special?

Many of the lessons or studies found on this site have been tried, tested, and refined for over a decade. I have used these tools on many occasions, and can confidently say that they are among the best ministry resources that are out there.
